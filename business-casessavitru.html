Business Cases

By Savitru Gadagkar

Introduction to Business Cases

This section discusses the business cases identified by Savitru Gadagkar in the context of SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities. The goal is to showcase how these cases align with the targets and indicators set by SDG 11 and contribute to building inclusive, resilient, and sustainable urban environments.

Business Case 1: Green Urban Spaces

This business case focuses on the development of green urban spaces to combat urban heat islands, improve air quality, and promote community well-being. Green spaces also contribute to sustainable cities by reducing carbon emissions and improving biodiversity.

Business Case 2: Sustainable Public Transport Systems

This case explores the implementation of sustainable public transport systems, including electric buses and shared mobility solutions, to reduce traffic congestion and carbon emissions in urban areas.

Business Case 3: Affordable and Resilient Housing

This business case addresses the need for affordable housing that is designed to withstand climate-related challenges, ensuring that urban residents have access to safe and sustainable living conditions.

Business Case 4: Smart Infrastructure for Efficient Cities

This case highlights the use of smart technologies such as IoT, data analytics, and automation to improve the efficiency of urban services, including waste management, water supply, and energy usage.
