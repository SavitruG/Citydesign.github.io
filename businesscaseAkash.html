Business Case: Budget and Infrastructure

Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

1. SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

Target: Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation.

This goal focuses on ensuring the development of infrastructure that is resilient, sustainable, and capable of supporting industrialization while also encouraging innovation in industries like construction, energy, and transportation.

  • Indicator 9.1.1: Proportion of the rural population who live within 2 km of an all-season road.
  • Indicator 9.1.2: Passenger and freight volumes, by mode of transport.

2. SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

Target: Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.

  • Indicator 11.1.1: Proportion of the urban population living in slums, informal settlements, or inadequate housing.
  • Indicator 11.6.1: Proportion of municipal solid waste collected and managed in controlled facilities out of total municipal solid waste generated.

Business Case: Travel and Tourism

Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

1. SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

Target: Promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all.

  • Indicator 8.9.1: Tourist arrivals by country of origin.
  • Indicator 8.9.2: Proportion of jobs in sustainable tourism industries.

2. SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

Target: Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.

  • Indicator 12.b.1: Number of countries implementing sustainable tourism policies and practices.
  • Indicator 12.c.1: Amount of resources used per unit of GDP.
