Course Introduction

What is the course about?

This course delves into the fundamental principles and techniques used in designing and analyzing algorithms. It focuses on problem-solving strategies, complexity analysis, and algorithmic paradigms like divide and conquer, dynamic programming, and greedy algorithms.

Why is the course important?

Understanding algorithms is critical for efficient problem-solving in computer science and engineering. This course equips students with skills to design optimized solutions, improve computational efficiency, and apply these concepts to real-world scenarios.

What are the expected outcomes?

By the end of this course, students will be able to analyze algorithm performance, apply advanced algorithmic techniques, and develop solutions for complex problems in various domains like data science, machine learning, and systems design.
