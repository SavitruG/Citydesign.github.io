Business Case: Pollution and Waste Management in Factories

Factory Sustainability: Managing Pollution and Waste

Target 12.4: Achieving the sound management of chemicals and all wastes throughout their life cycle, minimizing impacts on human health and the environment. Pollution and waste generated by industries need careful management and reduction. Factories can contribute significantly to sustainable practices by effectively managing and reducing waste through optimized operations and technology.

Target 9.4: By 2030, upgrade infrastructure and retrofit industries to make them sustainable, with enhanced resource-use efficiency and greater adoption of cleaner and greener technologies.
