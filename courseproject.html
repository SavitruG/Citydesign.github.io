City Design Overview

We were given the task of designing a smart city spanning 1500 sq km, with a focus on being realistic, practical, and future-ready.

Our journey began with a thorough analysis of the geography, weather conditions, and geological aspects of the area. This helped us create a city layout that aligns with natural resources and supports efficient urban living.

The budget was carefully planned across three stages, ensuring smooth execution and financial transparency throughout the project.

Aryavarta is built on the pillars of sustainability, innovation, and growth. Every aspect, from infrastructure to resource management, was designed thoughtfully to meet these goals.

This city represents a vision for the future, brought to life through dedicated teamwork, strategic planning, and a shared commitment to excellence. Aryavarta stands as a blueprint for modern, smart, and sustainable urban development.

Aryavarta

Aryavarta is a visionary smart city spanning 1500 sq km, comparable in size to Delhi, meticulously designed to embody sustainability, efficiency, and innovation. It seamlessly integrates cutting-edge technology, green infrastructure, and human-centric urban planning to create a balanced ecosystem for residents, businesses, and nature.
