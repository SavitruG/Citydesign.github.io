City Design Overview

We were given the task of designing a smart city spanning 1500 sq km, with a focus on being realistic, practical, and future-ready.

Our journey began with a thorough analysis of the geography, weather conditions, and geological aspects of the area. This helped us create a city layout that aligns with natural resources and supports efficient urban living.

Aryavarta is built on the pillars of sustainability, innovation, and growth. Every aspect, from infrastructure to resource management, was designed thoughtfully to meet these goals.

Aryavarta

Aryavarta is a visionary smart city near the city of Bengaluru, designed for sustainability, efficiency, and innovation. The city integrates technology, green infrastructure, and strategic urban planning for a balanced ecosystem.
